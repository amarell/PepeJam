What is PepeJam?

  • Free Music

    The purpose of this website is to enable the user the easy access to all the music they might possibly want. Music is great , and we need to use the technology as simple as possible for everyone on the Internet to enjoy it the way they always wanted to.

  • Save time

    PepeJam is designed to be very user-friendly and easy to use.

    It is also extremely fast and responsive, so the user won't waste time.

  • Save money

    Our app is one of the cheapest of the sort on the market, while the quality is on par, if not better, than other apps.

  • No ads

    For now, we won't be including ads on our app, because at PepeJam, user experience and quality is valued above the monetary gains. You can enjoy listening to your favourite playlists freely, without annoying ads to ruin your mood.

  • Personalized recommendations

    The website will gather data on which songs you like, and using some advanced algorithms recommend you similar music. Every week, we will make a personalized playlist, which you can give a listen to. This way, you will be able to discover new music, and never run out of it. Of course if you don't want us to collect your data, you can disable this by clicking on settings below.
